Novu gives you everything you need to build, manage, and deliver notifications across every channel — without the complexity of building it yourself. Connect an embeddable in-app Inbox to your frontend in minutes, define multi-step workflows visually or in code, and route messages through your preferred providers.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your Novu account">
    Sign up at [dashboard.novu.co](https://dashboard.novu.co/auth/sign-up) — the free tier is enough to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the Inbox to your app">
    Install `@novu/nextjs` (or `@novu/react`) and drop the `<Inbox />` component into your layout. See the [Next.js Quickstart](/quickstart/nextjs).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a workflow">
    Open the Novu dashboard, click **Workflows → Create workflow**, add an In-App step, and trigger it. Your notification appears in the Inbox instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add more channels">
    Connect SendGrid, Twilio, Slack, or any other provider in the [Integration Store](/integrations/overview) and add extra steps to your workflow.
  </Step>
</Steps>
